### Compress a file/folder
```sh
tar -cjf <zip_file_name>.tar.bz2 /path/to/folder/or/file
```

### Uncompress a file/folder
```sh
tar -xf <zip_file_name>.tar.bz2 /optional/folder/path/to/keep/unzip/content
```
